Meets

Our team hosts our meets at Timberstone middle school's field. In one season there are 8 meets.There are 2 diffrent kinds of meets, there are invitationals and meets for everyone.For an invitational meet there is only a certain amount of team members for each event that can go.In the other meets the whole team goes and participates.
